Deloitte Studio Jam

Problem Statement: How might we increase college football viewership (in-person and on tv/internet)?

Step 1: We discussed relevant topics surrounding college football and decided that we would focus on tickets, friends, and tailgates.

Step 2: We decided to focus on the University of Michigan for this example and specifically wanted to target students. We began by creating two user personas. These people represented students who we believed could be potential users of our product.

Step 3: We recognized that often times students who have season tickets end up missing games for various reasons, but do not sell their tickets because of inconveninces.

Step 4: We began brainstorming ways to increase viewership in this realm and decided to create a ticket exchange platform.
