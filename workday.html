Workday Research Jam

Problem Statement: Find major pain points in course registration for college students

Step 1: Interview a student to figure out personal pain points during course registration

Step 2: Come together with a group of people who had also interviewed students. Collaborate to figure out common pain points

Step 3: Create a post-it board of common pain points. Combine under umbrella statements.
