VROOM DESIGN JAM

Problem Statement: How can we make the mobile products webpage better?

Step 1: Browsed the mobile site and brainstormed a few areas that could use improvement. We came up with a few different ideas like eliminating the sticky header and footer they were using (as this took up about 1/3 of the already small size of a mobile phone screen), condensing information, and decreasing the amount of scrolling needed to reach important information.

Step 2: Focused in on a few of our ideas and began to sketch. We started with the main view as a product is chosen.

Step 3: Moved down the page following the natural flow and began to sketch again. For this view we chose to organize features in a more condenced way to decrease the amount of scrolling users have to do.

Step 4: Added headings to different features to group information. This would allow users to search easily for the car features they desire.

Step 5: Given that we only had 1 hour to implement a change in the platform, we were unable to include all of our ideas. Therefore, we wrote down a few other areas where we felt changes could be made.
